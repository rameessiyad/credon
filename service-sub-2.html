Ask an Advisor
As businesses grow and evolve, so do their financial needs. During periods of strong cash flow, trade and working capital finance may seem secondary. But when business slows, having access to the right financing lines becomes a key advantage. At Credon, we work closely with businesses to ensure they are prepared for every phase of the business cycle. Our role is to facilitate access to working capital and trade finance solutions offered by leading banks and financial institutions across the UAE and beyond. Working capital financing is not used to acquire long-term assets like property or machinery—instead, it supports your day-to-day operational requirements, including purchases, supplier payments, and customer invoicing.
